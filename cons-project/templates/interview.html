Interview Preparation Guide

Interview Tips

  • Research the company thoroughly, including their mission, values, and recent projects.
  • Practice common interview questions and prepare your answers.
  • Dress appropriately for the role and the company culture.
  • Arrive on time or a few minutes early to show punctuality.
  • Bring multiple copies of your resume, a notepad, and a pen.
  • Be confident, but not arrogant. Maintain good posture and eye contact.
  • Ask insightful questions about the role and the company.
  • Send a thank-you note or email after the interview to express your appreciation.
